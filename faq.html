FAQFrequently Asked Questions




  • How do I save a tile to my favorites?



    In the aggregator, drag your favorite tile to the "My Favs" link on the top bar. Note that weather tiles can't be saved.




  • How do I delete a tile from my favorites?



    Drag the tile you wish to delete to the "Delete" label on the top bar.




  • I've been waiting for 15 seconds, how come no tiles are loading?




    Refresh the page and try again.




  • My location isn't accurate, how can I change it?


    Under the Quick Settings menu in the aggregator, enter a new location by clicking the option to change your location.



  • How can I view news from other topics, such as Business or Technology?


    You can customize the news tiles by going into the Quick Settings and customizing as many news topics as you wish to view.




  • How can I view more than 1 tile from each source?


    Under the Show More Tiles option in the Quick Settings menu, you can change the number of tiles shown for each source.



  • How come I can't change the number of tiles shown for the Weather and Wikipedia tiles?


    It wouldn't make sense to show more than 1 since the weather tile is based on location and the Wikipedia shows its photo of the day.




  • What are the sources for all of these tiles?



    500px, The Hype Machine, Google News, Vimeo, Wikipedia, World Weather Online, Yelp, Youtube
